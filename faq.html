Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about our process, physicians, and services.

Who are the reviewing physicians?
All cases are reviewed by U.S. board-certified hematologists and oncologists. They have trained at leading academic medical centers and possess deep expertise in specific cancer types.
How long does the review process take?
Once we receive all necessary medical records, our standard turnaround time is 3 to 5 business days. We understand the urgency of cancer care and strive to provide insights as quickly as possible without compromising thoroughness.
How do I submit my medical records?
You can upload your records directly through our secure, HIPAA-compliant portal when you start your case review. Alternatively, you can sign a release form allowing our team to request the records directly from your healthcare providers on your behalf.
Will you communicate with my current doctor?
Yes, if you authorize us to do so. We believe in collaborative care and are happy to share our report and discuss our findings directly with your treating oncologist to ensure seamless integration of our recommendations into your care plan.
Do you prescribe medication or order tests?
No. Cancer Clarity MD provides advisory services and second opinions. We do not establish a primary doctor-patient relationship, prescribe medications, or order diagnostic tests. Our recommendations are meant to be discussed with and implemented by your local treating physician.
What role does AI play in your service?
We use advanced AI technology strictly to organize, summarize, and extract key data points from voluminous medical records. This allows our physicians to spend their time analyzing the clinical nuances of your case rather than sorting through paperwork. The final review, clinical judgment, and recommendations are always made by a human expert oncologist.
Do you accept international patients?
Yes, we provide expert reviews for patients worldwide. However, all medical records must be translated into English prior to submission. Please note that our recommendations are based on U.S. standards of care and FDA-approved therapies, which may differ from what is available in your home country.
Is my medical information secure?
Absolutely. We employ enterprise-grade encryption and strict security protocols. Our entire platform and all processes are fully compliant with HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act) regulations to ensure your protected health information remains confidential.
Can you help me find a clinical trial?
Yes. As part of our comprehensive review, our experts evaluate your specific diagnosis, treatment history, and genomic profile to identify relevant, currently enrolling clinical trials that may be appropriate for your situation.
What if my cancer type isn't listed on your site?
While we list the most common conditions we cover, our network includes sub-specialists for nearly all solid tumors and hematologic malignancies, including rare cancers. Please contact us or submit an inquiry, and we will confirm if we have the appropriate expert for your specific diagnosis.
Do you review pediatric cancer cases?
Currently, Cancer Clarity MD focuses exclusively on adult oncology and hematology cases (patients aged 18 and older). We do not provide reviews for pediatric cancers at this time.

Still have questions?

Our care coordination team is here to help you understand how our services can benefit your specific situation.
