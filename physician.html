LEAD PHYSICIAN

Dr. Nikki Bajaj, MD

Dr. Nikki Bajaj is a triple board-certified physician specializing in medical oncology, hematology, and internal medicine. With extensive clinical experience across diverse healthcare settings, she founded Cancer Clarity MD to address a critical gap in modern cancer care: the need for unhurried, deeply personalized, and technologically advanced second opinions.

Throughout her career, Dr. Bajaj has observed that the rapid pace of standard medical systems often leaves patients feeling overwhelmed and uncertain about their treatment plans. She believes that a cancer diagnosis requires more than just a brief consultation - it demands a comprehensive review of a patient's entire clinical history, molecular profile, and personal goals.

Expertise & Focus

Dr. Bajaj's clinical focus centers on precision oncology - utilizing advanced genomic testing (NGS) and molecular profiling to identify targeted therapies and immunotherapies tailored to the individual characteristics of a patient's tumor.

She maintains a rigorous commitment to staying at the forefront of oncology research, continuously monitoring updates to NCCN guidelines, ASCO/ASH presentations, and emerging clinical trials. By integrating proprietary AI tools into her review process, she ensures that no potential therapeutic option is overlooked, while applying her seasoned clinical judgment to determine what is truly appropriate for each patient.

The Concierge Approach

At Cancer Clarity MD, Dr. Bajaj personally reviews, validates, and signs every second opinion report. She is dedicated to providing clear, actionable guidance that empowers patients and their local treating oncologists to make the most informed decisions possible.

YOUR DEDICATED GUIDE

Patient Care Coordinator

Navigating medical records across different health systems can be exhausting. Your dedicated Patient Care Coordinator handles this burden for you - from the moment you initiate a case review to the day you receive your report.

Your coordinator serves as your single point of contact. They manage the secure collection of your clinical notes, pathology reports, and imaging, ensuring the reviewing physician has a complete and accurate clinical picture before analysis begins. They also coordinate your consultation scheduling and are always available to answer logistical questions.

  • Manages secure collection of all medical records on your behalf
  • Coordinates scheduling for your video consultation with the physician
  • Available to answer logistical questions throughout the process
  • Ensures nothing is missing before the physician begins their review
