CONSULTATION OPTIONS

Our Services

Each case is reviewed individually by a U.S. board-certified specialist.

Written Second Opinion

A comprehensive written review of your submitted medical records, conducted by a U.S. board-certified hematologist or oncologist.

Includes

  • AI-assisted record organization
  • Review by board-certified specialist
  • Detailed written report
  • Answers to your specific questions
  • Delivered within 48 to 72 hours of record confirmation

Written Report + Video Consultation

Includes the written second opinion plus a secure video consultation with the reviewing physician to discuss findings directly.

Includes

  • Everything in the Written Second Opinion
  • 45-minute secure video consultation
  • Direct discussion of findings and questions
  • Video recording provided upon request

Fees are discussed individually based on the complexity and scope of each case. To inquire, please contact us at contact@cancerclaritymd.com
